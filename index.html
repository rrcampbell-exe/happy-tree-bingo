Happy Tree Bingo

How to Play

  1. Tune in to the Hence Fort Twitch stream on Fridays.
  2. When Hence says or does anything associated with a prospective bingo space, he'll call out the bingo space in question.
  3. If you have that bingo space on your card, click it to turn it yellow!
  4. Once you have a complete row, column, or diagonal, screenshot your bingo card, post it in the #bingo-submissions channel in the Hence Fort Discord, and let Hence know in the Twitch chat!
  5. If Hence confirms you've won, you'll have the chance to join him on air for a future Stardew Valley community farm stream!
