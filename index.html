How to Play

1. Tune In

Tune in to the Hence Fort Twitch stream on Fridays in order to play.

2. Listen for Bingo Spaces

When Hence calls out a bingo space, mark it on your card by clicking it.

3. Complete a Row, Column, or Diagonal

If you think you have a bingo, post your card in the #bingo-submissions channel in the Hence Fort Discord, and let Hence know in the Twitch chat!

4. Win?!?!?

If Hence confirms you've won, you'll have the chance to join him on air for a future Stardew Valley community farm stream!
